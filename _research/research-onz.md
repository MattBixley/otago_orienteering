# ONZ Resource Link Catalog

Curated links to **Orienteering New Zealand (ONZ)** resources for the rebuilt Otago
Orienteering club website. Strategy: **do not build our own training/how-to pages —
link out to these authoritative ONZ (and IOF) pages instead.**

- Source site: <https://www.orienteering.org.nz/>
- Verified: 2026-07-15. All `orienteering.org.nz` URLs below returned HTTP 200.
- IOF links (`orienteering.sport`) are valid pages but block automated HEAD checks
  (return 403 to bots); they load fine in a browser.

## Notes for site build

- **Beginners page** → embed/link "Get started", "Intro to orienteering", "Choose the right course".
- **Learn/Training page** → link the Coaching and Training Hub as the primary CTA; secondary links to workbooks and the "Leg by leg" video playlist.
- **Map symbols / control descriptions** → link the simplified ISOM/ISSprOM legends and the Maprunner control-description guide (best for beginners); link full/official IOF specs for reference.
- **Schools page** → link Intro to schools + Schools Knowledge Hub + Kiwi-O manual.
- **Safety page** → link ONZ Health & Safety and the accident-reporting form.
- Google Drive / Google Docs links are ONZ-hosted originals; consider they may change — prefer linking the parent ONZ page (Coaching & Training Hub, Health & Safety) rather than deep Drive links where stability matters.
