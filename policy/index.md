This page brings together the policies that guide how Otago Orienteering Club
operates. Good policies protect our members, our volunteers and our sport.

## Health &amp; Safety

*[Placeholder — to be completed by the committee.]*

The club is committed to running safe events and managing risk in line with the
Health and Safety at Work Act 2015. Our approach follows the national guidance
provided by Orienteering New Zealand:

- [ONZ Health &amp; Safety / Risk Management](https://www.orienteering.org.nz/health-safety/)
- [ONZ Health and Safety Policy summary](https://www.orienteering.org.nz/wp-content/uploads/2018/05/A3-Health-Safety.pdf) (PDF)
- [Report an incident (ONZ Accident Reporting form)](https://forms.gle/fi1WYaALe7bUkGxa8)

This section should describe how the club identifies hazards, briefs
participants, manages event-day safety, and responds to incidents and missing
persons.
