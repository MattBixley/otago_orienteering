We would love to hear from you — whether you have a question, want to join, or
are just curious about giving orienteering a try.

## Get in touch

For general enquiries, email **[committee@dunedinorienteering.org](mailto:committee@dunedinorienteering.org)**.
We are a volunteer-run club, so please allow a little time for a reply.

**Post:** P.O. Box 969, Dunedin, Otago 9054, New Zealand.

<div class="callout">
  <h3>You do not need to contact us first</h3>
  <p>Anyone can simply turn up to an event and have a go — no membership, no
  booking, no experience needed. Check what is on at our
  <a href="/events/">events page</a> and come along.</p>
</div>

## How to join

Membership gets you half-price entry at Club and OY events, plus certificate
eligibility, junior training-camp subsidies, coaching clinics and social
events. Categories and fees are set out in our
[Membership &amp; fees policy](/policy/).

Join or renew online through RevolutioniseSPORT:

- **Register:** [revolutionise.com.au/dunedinoc/registration](https://www.revolutionise.com.au/dunedinoc/registration/)
- **Log in:** [revolutionise.com.au/dunedinoc/login](https://www.revolutionise.com.au/dunedinoc/login/)

Questions about joining? Email
**[membership@dunedinorienteering.org](mailto:membership@dunedinorienteering.org)**.
