A fast, spectator-friendly sprint on the tracks and terraces of Signal Hill,
looking out over the city and harbour. Great for first-timers: the short course
sticks to obvious tracks and features.

## Getting there

Meet at the car park at [assembly location]. Parking is limited, so carpool
where you can. Follow the orienteering signs from the road.

## What to bring

Shoes with grip, weather-appropriate layers, and a drink. We'll have spare
compasses to borrow and someone on hand to walk beginners through it.

> First event? Arrive by 9:15am and ask for the newcomers' briefing at the
> registration tent.
