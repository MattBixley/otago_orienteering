Our classic bush event in the native and pine of Whare Flat — technical
contour and vegetation navigation for those wanting a challenge, plus easier
tracked courses for newcomers and juniors.

## Courses

- **White / Yellow** — beginner-friendly, following tracks and clear features.
- **Orange** — a step up, leaving the tracks with route choice.
- **Red / hard** — full technical bush navigation for experienced runners.

## Notes

Full leg cover recommended in the bush. Whistles required on the harder
courses — ask at registration if you need one.
