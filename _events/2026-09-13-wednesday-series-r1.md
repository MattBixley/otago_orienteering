The first of our six-round spring twilight series — short, social urban and
park events on weekday evenings. Perfect for building skills over the season.
Run it hard as a training hit-out, or walk it with the kids.

## The series

Six rounds through spring, scored cumulatively — your best four count toward
the series standings. Come to as many or as few as you like.

## Format

Start any time in the window, follow the course, and we'll pack up at 6:45pm.
Bring a headtorch for the later rounds as the light drops.
